Exercise 7: Image Treatments

Details

Create a 6 post image carousel for a lecture series. The first image will be the title of the lecture series, overall timing, and location then the other 5 images will show an image with text displaying the lecturer name and date. Come up with a graphic treatment for the photographs that unifies them and makes them feel like they belong together. Each image in the carousel is 1080px by 1080px. You will pick at least 5 of your assigned photographs. The lecture series, location, and dates are up to you but the lecturer is the name of the person's photograph you are using.

  1. Make sure your 10 photographs are jpgs.
  2. Name them 01 through 10, first photograph is 01.jpg and the last is 10.jpg.
  3. Upload your appropriately named 10 photographs to this folder.
  4. You will be assigned a number 1 through 10, download all (or at least 5) of the photographs with that number from this folder.

Submission Requirements

Six jpg images for the carousel uploaded to Canvas, named 01.jpg to 06.jpg.
