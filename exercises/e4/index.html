Exercise 4: Taste Zine

Details

Make a 12 page booklet that shows design that you appeals to you. The designs you choose can be any form but try and show a variety (for example, not all logos). The booklet needs a front cover, which should have a title and your name on it, on page 1 and a back cover on page 12. The designs you choose should go on pages 2 to 11 and should credit the designer or studio that made them in some way. Use the same document set up as Exercise 1, but this time you will have 12 pages instead of 1.

Submission Requirements

Submit a PDF of booklet as spreads on Canvas and bring the printed, folded, and assembled booklet to class next week.
