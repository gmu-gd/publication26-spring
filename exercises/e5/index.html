Exercise 5: The Spread

Details

Find 5 examples of the first spread of an article in a magazine. Choose different magazines, different types of magazines, and different types of articles. The examples should treat the two pages individual pages as a spread. For each example write a few sentences as to why you picked it and what you think it working well in the design.

Place your examples and text in a 5 page 17x11 PDF document.
